= ABOUT US =


Cafe Novus has been serving the High Abbey community since 1995 and is locally renowned for its highest standards for fresh, artisan food & beverages all expertly prepared on premise. Furthermore, our commitment to the community goes beyond our menu. 5% of every purchase made at Cafe Novus is donated to the American Red Cross. With a stellar reputation inside and outside our establishment, Cafe Novus is High Abbey's best local cafe.

What was once an abandoned residential property has been transformed into a beautiful, spacious cafe & delicatessen that has attracted patrons from all over New Jersey. We want every customer who enters our premise to feel invited to stay not only to enjoy our beverages and food but also to relax and enjoy the overall experience of Cafe Novus. Enjoy at no additional cost:

  • High speed wifi


  • Study section equipped with outlets for media devices


  • Lounge seating and tables


  • Free coffee and tea refills
